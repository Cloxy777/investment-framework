# Upgrade 1 — Owner Earnings Adjustment 🔴 CRITICAL

For businesses where Growth CapEx > 30% of total CapEx, use Owner Earnings instead of reported FCF:

**Owner Earnings = Net Income + D&A − Maintenance CapEx only**

Apply to FCF Yield scoring (not reported FCF). Flag in analysis notes.

Affected businesses: Microsoft (Azure), Alphabet, Meta, Amazon.

Impact on scoring:

- MSFT: FCF yield 2.3% → ~4.0% → Score 6 → Score 5 (Hold, not Trim)
- META: 3.1% → ~3.8% → partial improvement toward Score 4

Academic basis: Buffett 1986 shareholder letter on Owner Earnings. Capital intensity studies show raw FCF understates moat-building investment value.

# Upgrade 2 — Historical PE Relative Modifier 🔴 CRITICAL

Add PE Regime Modifier to valuation scoring, applied after quality gate passes:

| Condition | Score Adjustment |
| --- | --- |
| Forward PE > 20% below 10yr historical avg | −1 |
| Within ±10% of 10yr avg | No change |
| Forward PE > 20% above 10yr avg | +1 |

May 2026 examples:

- MSFT: 24.9× vs 31× (10yr avg) = −19.6% → Score −1
- META: 22.1× vs 27× (5yr avg) = −18% → borderline
- AAPL: 37× vs 24.5× (10yr avg) = +51% → Score +1

Academic basis: Mean-reversion studies on large-cap quality. Berkshire performance analysis shows PE-discount-to-own-history as reliable entry signal.

# Upgrade 3 — PEG Ratio for Fast Growers 🟠 HIGH

Apply ONLY to Lynch Fast Grower category (EPS growth > 15% for 3+ consecutive years).

⚠️ Never apply to cyclicals, stalwarts, or turnarounds.

| PEG Ratio | Score Modifier |
| --- | --- |
| < 0.8 | −1 |
| 0.8–1.2 | No change |
| 1.2–1.8 | +0.5 |
| > 2.0 | +1 |

May 2026: META PEG 0.90 → Score −1 → Full position 6–8%.

Academic basis: Lynch Magellan 29.2% CAGR (1977–1990). PEG studies 1999–2010 showed consistent outperformance for fast growers.

# Upgrade 4 — Turnaround Sub-Gate (Fallen Angel Path) 🟠 HIGH

A business failing 2–4 quality criteria may enter as Conditional Watch (2–3% max) if ALL five conditions are met:

1. ROIC historically > 15% for ≥5 years in the past decade
2. CEO or CFO insider buying > $500K in past 6 months (SEC Form 4 verified)
3. At least one credible independent FV estimate shows ≥40% MOS to current price
4. Net Debt/EBITDA < 3×
5. Core moat still identifiable (brand, network, or switching cost — even if under pressure)

Position rules:

- Max 2–3% only. No exceptions.
- Mandatory 2-quarter review: exit if ROIC not trending toward 12%+.
- Full position only when original quality gate fully re-passes.

May 2026 — NKE qualifies: 5yr ROIC history 25–35%, CEO bought $1M+ at $42 (Apr 2026), Morningstar FV $74.97 (57% MOS), Net Debt/EBITDA 1.1×, brand moat present. → Conditional Watch 2–3%. Exit trigger: gross margin < 43% by Q2 FY2027.

# Upgrade 5 — Debt Gate Business Model Context 🟡 MEDIUM

For payment networks, exchanges, and asset-light businesses where 100% of debt is financial (buyback funding, not operational need), adjust threshold to Net Debt/EBITDA < 4×, provided:

- Interest coverage > 15×
- Debt rated Investment Grade (BBB+ or better)
- Zero credit risk in core business model

Standard 2.5× threshold unchanged for all manufacturing, retail, commodity, and services businesses.

May 2026: MA Net Debt/EBITDA 2.5× → borderline FAIL → clean PASS under adjusted threshold.

# Upgrade 6 — Momentum Filter as Entry Confirmation 🟡 MEDIUM

Soft modifier only — does not override quality gate assessment.

- Stock above 200-day MA → proceed with entry as scored
- Stock below 200-day MA → require one additional confirmation before entry:
    - Earnings beat > 5% vs consensus, OR
    - New insider buying event > $250K (CEO/CFO), OR
    - Analyst upgrade with price target > 30% above current

Academic basis: Factor research (BlackRock, Fama-French 5-factor) shows adding momentum filter to quality+value reduces value trap risk by ~40%.

May 2026:

- META, MSFT: above 200MA → proceed normally
- V: below 200MA → await $275–285 AND one confirmation trigger
- NKE: below 200MA → turnaround triggers already required; momentum adds further discipline

# Upgrade 7 — Position Cap Validation (for Master Framework reference) 🔵

This framework's 6–8% single-position cap is empirically correct. Do not increase it.

Verdad research (10,000 simulated portfolios): hyper-concentrated portfolios of 5–10 stocks trail diversified quality portfolios by 1–2% annually due to volatility drag — even when stock selection is correct. Best ideas outperform by 1.6–2.6% per quarter; broader portfolios of 15–25 capture factor premiums more reliably.
