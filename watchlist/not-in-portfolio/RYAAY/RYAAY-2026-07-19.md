| Term | Meaning |

| **Ancillary revenue** | An airline's non-ticket revenue (baggage fees, priority boarding, reserved seats, etc.) — Ryanair's rose 6% to €4.99BN in FY26. |

| **Hard disqualifier** | A Quality Score condition that fails a company regardless of weighted score; none fired for RYAAY. |
