## Glossary

See [framework/glossary.md](../../../framework/glossary.md) for the standing definitions file. Terms used in this addendum (beyond those already covered by the 06-20/06-22/06-24 session logs' glossaries):

- **CapEx (Capital Expenditure)** — Money spent acquiring or upgrading physical assets (here, fab/manufacturing capacity); the "growth-capex" carve-out in the FCF/NI hard disqualifier exists because heavy capex can suppress FCF/NI even in a healthy, expanding business.
- **Class-action lawsuit** — A lawsuit filed by one or more plaintiffs on behalf of a larger group with the same claim; an open, unresolved legal risk flag, not a settled finding.
- **EBIT / EBITDA** — Earnings Before Interest and Taxes / before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization.
- **Effective tax rate** — Tax provision ÷ pretax income for a given period; used here to compute NOPAT from EBIT.
- **EV/EBIT** — Enterprise Value ÷ EBIT; one of the two criteria that flipped from FAIL to PASS this session as real Q3 FY2026 data replaced a stale TTM.
- **Floor price** — The guaranteed minimum price set in a take-or-pay contract, protecting the seller's margin even if spot/market prices later fall.
- **GAAP** — Generally Accepted Accounting Principles.
- **Hard disqualifier** — A Quality Score condition that fails a company regardless of its weighted score; MU's "not FCF-positive 3+ consecutive years" fires unconditionally this session.
- **Invested Capital** — Debt + equity (net of cash) put to work in the business; the ROIC denominator.
- **NOPAT** — Net Operating Profit After Tax, the ROIC numerator.
- **Quality Score** — This framework's 0-100.0 continuous quality grade; a company must score 80.0+ to proceed to Phase 02. MU scores 57.0.
- **RPO (Remaining Performance Obligations)** — The dollar value of contracted-but-not-yet-recognized revenue; cited here for MU's ~$100B in SCA-backed RPO.
- **Rule 0** — Always fetch a live price before any valuation work.
- **Rule 9** — The framework's list of events that force an immediate re-valuation: earnings, guidance revision, management change, M&A, macro shift, or a >15% unexplained price move.
- **SCA (Strategic Customer Agreement)** / **Take-or-pay** — See [glossary.md](../../../framework/glossary.md) for full definitions; added there this session.
- **TTM (Trailing Twelve Months)** — The most recent 12 months of reported results.
